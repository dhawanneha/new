Cappex

Redesigning a website to help high school students search for colleges



Methods

User Interviews, Comparative Analysis, Heuristic Evaluation, Surveys, Persona, Sketching, Paper Prototyping, & Low Fidelity Prototype

Duration

12 weeks

Team

Crystal Shan, Juliana Lam, Neha Dhawan, Yilin Xue, & Yuan Lee



Role

UX Researcher & Designer


Problem Statement

Cappex experiences lower rate of engagement as compared to its competitors and wants to evaluate its search functionality.

    All team members then came up with wireframes based on our research. The five wireframes were combined into two main themes:

    • User logins and then the search results are displayed based on their profile information.
    • Users don't have to login and search has useful filters to narrow results.

    After getting feedback, we decided to focus on the second theme where user can explore the website without logging in.
